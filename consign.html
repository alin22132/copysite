  • National leader in Vintage Auto Sales selling OVER 1,000 vintage cars a year!
  • WWVA will sell your vehicle for 20% more than comparable private party sales.

  • We sell 95% of our vehicles online. So rest assured your vehicle will most likely never be driven or have the “tires kicked”.

FAQ

How much does it cost to consign?

$2,500 or 12% of the selling price, per vehicle. 

What fees are included and how much does it cost to consign?

$500 inspection fee and any shipping costs associated with getting the vehicle to our facility. NO UP FRONT FEES means that we settle up when the vehicle sells, thus no out of pocket cost for you prior.

Does my car have to be in WWVA's possession?

Yes, the vehicle will need to be on site and inside of our 80,000 sq ft showroom. We assume full responsibility for the sale.

How long is the contract period?

60 days, our goal is to move your vehicle in a matter of weeks!

What if my car doesn't sell?

In the rare event that a vehicle doesn’t sell you have the option to take it back home at the end of our 60 day contract.
